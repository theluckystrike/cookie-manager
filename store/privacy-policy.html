Privacy Policy

Cookie Manager Chrome Extension

Last updated: February 11, 2026

TL;DR

  • We collect nothing
  • Your cookies stay on your device
  • We make zero network requests
  • The code is open source

Our Commitment

Cookie Manager is built by Zovo with privacy as a core principle. We believe developer tools should work entirely locally without any data collection.

Data We Collect

None. Cookie Manager does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

Specifically, we do NOT:

  • Send your cookies to any server
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Use analytics or telemetry
  • Store data outside your browser
  • Make any network requests

What We Do Access

Cookie Manager accesses the following data locally:

  • Cookies: Read and displayed only in the popup interface
  • Current Tab URL: Used only to filter cookies by domain
  • Local Settings: Your preferences stored in browser storage

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Permissions Explained

cookies

Required to read, create, modify, and delete cookies. This is the core functionality of the extension.

activeTab

Required to get the current tab's URL so we can show relevant cookies.

storage

Required to save your preferences (like read-only mode) locally on your device.

host_permissions

Required to access cookies for any website you visit. This allows the extension to work on all sites.

contextMenus

Adds right-click menu options to quickly clear or export cookies for the current site without opening the popup.

notifications

Shows brief confirmation messages when cookies are cleared or exported via the context menu.

alarms

Powers auto-delete rules and periodic maintenance tasks that run in the background on a schedule you configure.
